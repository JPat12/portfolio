"Good decisions come from experience and experience comes from bad decisions"

Hey, it's me, Pat! I'm an aspiring Software Engineer from Quezon City and a licensed Mechanical Engineer. Over the years I've been seeking what I truly desire in life. I've been doing a lot of work in every field that I've encountered. I gave my best in every decision I've been making. As I aged, I realized that having bad decisions gave me experiences that would help me be a better person and would benefit me to having good decisions in life.

At first, I thought I would end up and do things all my life with the profession that I've taken up when I was in college, however, based on my past experiences I decided to pursue this career. I love building things. I like logically solving things. I love to analyze things and I always make it a habit to solve and meet the needs of my clients.

In this portfolio, you can see what I've gained in my software engineering journey over the past few weeks. In the future, I aspire to be one of the best developers in town. Not seeking only for the betterment of my career, but also the success of my clients.
